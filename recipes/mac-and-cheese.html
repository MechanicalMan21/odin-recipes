Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

The Mac-and-Cheese is simply the most delicious of its kind. Its cheesiness flavor combined with the chewy rolls
make this a perfect delicacy for everyone in any occasion. It is not too difficult to make and for anyone that lacks
experience in cooking. All in all, the mac-and-cheese is the perfect go-to food if you ever feel that cheesy craving.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings: You could use any, but salt and pepper along with paprika is recommended
  • Bread Crumbs: You can also make your own

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir.
  3. Make the topping, spread it over macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown.
