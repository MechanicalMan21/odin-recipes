Stovetop Brown Rice

Description

The classic Brown Rice. A healthier alternative to white rice. It is packed with fiber to get your bowl running.
There are extra steps to making this. However, at the end, it ends working really well because of its flavor and texture.
It is best paired with veggies!

Ingredients

  • Just buy Brown Rice!

Steps

  1. Rinse rice under water. Its best to also soak rice for 30 minutes if you have time
  2. Add rice to pot
  3. Add water to pot. For every cup of rice, add 2 cups of water. The ratio should be 2:1
